Our Work

We intend to accomplish our goal of #PlasticFreeChilika within 2 years. We aspire to start this off with Gambhari, but scale this to other regions aswell, as quickly and efficiently as possible. Here is a small glimpse of the activities and work we have done to date.

Awareness Sessions

We strongly believe that awareness amongst the locals is the most essenstial step towards the change. Any project can not succeed without the support of the locals, and to gain that support, they must be well aware about the importance of the project and how it will benefit the natural ecosystem of the Chilika lake.

Meeting with the Chilika Development Authority

Concensus building lies at the core of impact. Our team had a meeting with the Chilika Development Authority to seek their support and guidance to help us achieve our goal. We need to unite at all levels to make #PlasticFreeChilika a reality.

Tree plantation

As the saying goes, "he who plants a tree, plants a hope for future". We planted 150+ saplings in a local high school at Gambhari (our pilot project) . This is to engage the local youth and inculcate within them a sense of love and responsbility towards mother earth. These saplings would futher help maintain the balance of the underground water table. When grown up with care, these trees would not only provide fruits, but also, shade for us to conduct our future sessions.

Bamboo Mug Distribution

Not only do we educate the youth about the adverse effects of plastic, but also, provide sustainable alternatives. Bamboo mugs are eco friendly, and a very handy alternative for paper/plastic mugs which harm the environment. Our junior rangers have imbibed the values of " small individualistic changes" in their own lifestyles, which would add up to fight a greater cause against pollution.

Online Upcycling Workshop

Reusing stuff is one of the key ways in which we can reduce waste generation. We encourge the volunteers to use their creative minds to explore new ways in which they can reuse things in their house. As students, we use alot of paper. So, we decided to upcycle used papers into beautiful handmade papers , which would serve as an amazing canvas for art of all forms. We conducted an online workshop to make handmade paper out of used sheets from notebooks, and ended up realising that we have some really good artists in our team.

Nomination in the Global Beyond Plastic Awards

We were honoured to be nominated by the Beyond Plastic Awards among the the top 10 projects world wide that strive towards a better society.
